## 3. Convex optimization (LP, QP, convex conjugate)

In *Mathematics for Machine Learning*, **linear programming**, **quadratic programming**, and the **convex conjugate** appear as material **inside** the single section §7.3 — they are **not** separate top-level section numbers in the book’s table of contents.

| Topic | Primary chapter / sections |
| --- | --- |
| Convex optimization (including linear and quadratic programming, convex conjugate) | **Chapter 7**, §7.3 *Convex Optimization* |

Read §7.3 fully and follow its internal structure for LP, QP, Legendre–Fenchel / convex conjugate, and related convexity tools used in machine learning.
