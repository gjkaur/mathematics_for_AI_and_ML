## Suggested reading order for Day 8

Read **Chapter 12** in order **§12.1 → §12.5**. The primal and dual formulations motivate kernels; **§12.4** rephrases the dual using kernel functions; **§12.5** covers how the optimization is solved in practice.

**Prerequisites:** **Chapter 3** (inner products, norms), **Chapter 7** §7.2–§7.3 (Lagrange multipliers, convex problems), **Chapter 12** builds directly on constrained optimization ideas from Part I.
