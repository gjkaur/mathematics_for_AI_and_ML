## Suggested reading order for Day 4

Read **Chapter 6** in order **§6.1 → §6.5**. Earlier sections define events, probability mass and density, and conditioning; later sections use those ideas for expectations, independence, and the Gaussian family used throughout Parts I and II of the book.

**Prerequisites:** basic set notation and calculus (integration for continuous random variables); **Chapter 5** is not strictly required for §6.1–§6.3 but helps when expectations and gradients appear together later.
